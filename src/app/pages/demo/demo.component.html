Live Demo

This sandbox demonstrates tamper-evident logging, write-only field protection, and selective shredding. All data shown is synthetic.

What this demo proves

  • ►Events are sealed into a tamper-evident chain that detects any modification
  • ►Sensitive fields are protected with write-only encryption that the service itself cannot reverse
  • ►Shredding makes protected data permanently irrecoverable without breaking the chain

How to use this demo

  1. Open the Logger and submit a test decision event
  2. Open the Verifier and confirm the chain is intact
  3. Open the Shredder, shred the record, then re-verify to confirm integrity is preserved

What to look for

  • ►After logging: the event appears with an entry hash linking it to the previous record
  • ►After verifying: the chain reports all hashes valid with zero errors
  • ►After shredding: sensitive fields show as irrecoverable, but the chain still verifies cleanly

This demo uses synthetic data. It demonstrates technical capabilities, not a compliance certification.
