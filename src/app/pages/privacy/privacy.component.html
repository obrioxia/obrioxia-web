2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, work email, company name, and authentication credentials.
  • Audit Log Data: Data payloads sent to our API for logging. We separate technical metadata from Personally Identifiable Information (PII) using our proprietary encryption architecture.
  • Usage Data: Information on how the Service is accessed and used (e.g., IP address, browser type, timestamps).

3. The Tamper-Evident Ledger & GDPR

Obrioxia operates on a Write-Once-Read-Many (WORM) architecture. To reconcile the permanence of the hash-chained ledger with GDPR "Right to Erasure" requirements, we utilise a crypto-shredding technique. PII is encrypted with write-only per-event keys before hashing. If you request deletion, we destroy the specific key material, rendering your personal data cryptographically irrecoverable while leaving the audit integrity intact.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Obrioxia Service.
  • Process and complete transactions, and send related information.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security breaches.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Audit logs are retained according to the tier of your subscription. Retention periods vary by plan.

6. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit and advanced envelope encryption for data at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
