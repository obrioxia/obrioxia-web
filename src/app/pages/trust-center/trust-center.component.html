Trust Center

We secure the record of what your AI did.

Obrioxia is built on a "Zero Trust" philosophy. We assume that systems can be compromised, and we design our ledger to provide forensic evidence even in adversarial conditions.

Encryption Standards

We utilise TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Data at rest is protected using envelope encryption with industry-standard authenticated ciphers. Our write-only crypto-shredding engine uses per-event sealing to ensure the production service can encrypt data but never decrypt historical records.

The Tamper-Evident Model

Our core security thesis is "Write Once, Read Many" (WORM). Once a decision ID is generated and secured via cryptographic hash-chaining, any alteration — by internal staff, your admins, or external attackers — breaks the chain and is detected by the Chain Verifier.

Privacy & Right to Erasure

We resolve the conflict between "Tamper-Evident Logs" and "Right to be Forgotten." By encrypting PII with write-only per-event keys before hashing, we allow for the cryptographic destruction of personal data. When a record's key material is destroyed, PII access is permanently revoked and the data is rendered cryptographically irrecoverable, while the audit trail remains intact and verifiable.

Data Residency & Retention

Obrioxia manages data retention through cryptographic access revocation to align with your legal jurisdiction. Retention periods vary by subscription tier. Contact us for details on long-term archival options.

Access Control Philosophy

Write-only protection for sensitive fields: the service cannot decrypt stored PII. Two authentication modes are supported: demo API keys for sandbox exploration, and Firebase Bearer tokens for production accounts. PDF generation and data access are ownership-verified — you can only retrieve records you created.
