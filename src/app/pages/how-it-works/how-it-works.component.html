How It Works

Layer 1: The Concept

Think of Obrioxia as a "Black Box" flight recorder for your AI fleet. Just as an aircraft recorder survives a crash to tell investigators what happened, Obrioxia creates a tamper-evident record of your AI's decision-making process. It provides the "Digital Alibi" you need when liability is in question.

Layer 2: The Mechanism

We do not use standard database logs which can be edited by admins. Instead, we use a cryptographic chain.

  1. Every event is converted into a strict, canonical format.
  2. We calculate a SHA-256 hash of that event combined with the hash of the previous event.
  3. This creates an unbroken chain. If anyone tries to delete or modify a record in the past, the mathematical link breaks, and the Verifier will flag the system as corrupted.

Layer 3: Enterprise Controls

While the chain is tamper-evident, the data payload handles privacy intelligently. Sensitive fields are encrypted with write-only keys before they enter the ledger. This allows you to retain the *proof* of a decision for regulatory purposes while cryptographically shredding the *personal details* of the user on request, addressing two conflicting regulatory requirements.
