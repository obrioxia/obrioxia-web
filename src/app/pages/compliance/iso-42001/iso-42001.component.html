Obrioxia for ISO/IEC 42001

Operational Control for AI Management Systems

ISO/IEC 42001 requires organizations to establish confident controls over their AI systems. Obrioxia supports the "AI System Impact Assessment" and "AI System Life Cycle" controls by providing a definitive operational history.

  • • Monitoring: Continuous verification of system integrity.
  • • Incident Management: Forensic reconstruction capabilities for post-incident analysis.
  • • Control: Granular retention and deletion policies to manage data lifecycle risks.
