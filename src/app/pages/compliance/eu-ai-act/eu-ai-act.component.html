Obrioxia for Article 12

Automated Record-Keeping for High-Risk AI

Article 12 of the EU AI Act mandates that high-risk AI systems must automatically generate logs to ensure traceability throughout the system's lifecycle. Obrioxia helps meet this requirement.

  • • Traceability: We log input data, output decisions, and system confidence scores.
  • • Auditability: Our hash-chain ensures logs cannot be tampered with post-factum.
  • • Accessibility: Evidence packs provide the necessary documentation for conformity assessments.

Note: Obrioxia provides the technical layer for compliance. We do not provide legal advice.
