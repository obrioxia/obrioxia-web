The Problem

AI/API bills are hard to defend. Provider dashboards only show high-level totals, and standard application logs are mutable.

  • ► Clients ask why spend happened and where the money went.
  • ► Teams need concrete evidence, not just screenshots of a billing dashboard.
  • ► You lack a clear internal, tamper-evident record of what workflow, team, or agent caused the spend.

How It Works

STEP 1

Proxy captures AI/API usage events

Intercepts every request seamlessly without altering upstream API calls.

STEP 2

Each spend event is sealed into a hash chain

Cryptographic payload includes all critical business and billing metadata.

STEP 3

Tampering breaks verification

Any silent modification to local records breaks the math instantly.

STEP 4

Usage can be reconciled against provider bills

Local evidence totals perfectly align with (or audit) provider invoices.

STEP 5

Signed evidence packs can be exported

Generate verifiable offline manifests for regulators and finance teams.

What It Proves

  • ► Existing sealed records cannot be silently modified without breaking verification.
  • ► The evidence trail can show model, workflow, team, tokens, estimated cost, and hashes.
  • ► Signed evidence packs can be verified independently offline.

What It Does Not Prove

  • ► It is not full WORM compliance unless S3 Object Lock witness anchoring is configured.
  • ► It does not prove the upstream provider definitively processed exact bytes.
  • ► It is not legal advice or a compliance guarantee.
  • ► It does not make local storage immune to total rewrite before external anchoring.
